The 404 Museum

The internet's best 404 pages, preserved and celebrated.

What's this?

The 404 Museum is a curated archive of beautifully designed 404 error pages from across the web. When most sites show a generic "Page Not Found" message, these ones treat the 404 as an opportunity for creativity, branding, and delight.

This museum preserves them so you can appreciate the craft that goes into making "nothing found" feel like something worth seeing.

How it works

  • Browse the gallery to discover creative 404 pages from well-known companies
  • Click any exhibit to see it in detail, along with metadata about when and who submitted it
  • Use the "I'm feeling curious" button to be surprised by a random exhibit
  • Filter by tags to find specific styles (minimal, illustration, glitch, etc.)

Submit your own

Found a beautiful 404 page that belongs in the museum? Submit it here and it will be reviewed before being added to the collection.
